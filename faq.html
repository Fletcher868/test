Commonly Asked Questions

Find quick answers about security, features, and your VeroNote account.

General

What is VeroNote?
VeroNote is a privacy-first, end-to-end encrypted note-taking platform. It uses "Zero-Knowledge" architecture, which ensures that only the account holder has the keys to decrypt and read the stored information.
Why choose VeroNote over others?

Most "secure" note apps only encrypt the text inside the note. At VeroNote, we believe privacy should be absolute.

  • Encrypted Metadata: Unlike competitors, we encrypt your Note Titles and Folder names. Even we don't know what you are writing about.
  • No-Data-Collection: We don't track your location, your device ID, or your usage habits.
  • Zero-Knowledge: Your password never touches our server. Encryption happens entirely in your browser.
  • Instant Portability: Export your entire vault in one click. You own your data, not us.
Is it really free?
Yes. Our Free plan includes unlimited notes and core E2EE security. We offer a Pro plan for users who need extended version history and advanced organizational tools.
Do you have a mobile app?
VeroNote is currently a Progressive Web App (PWA) optimized for mobile browsers. You can "Add to Home Screen" on iOS and Android for a native-like experience. Dedicated app store versions are in our future roadmap.

Security & Privacy

How exactly is my data protected?
We use the industry-standard AES-GCM 256-bit encryption. When you log in, your password creates a "Master Key" locally in your browser. This key is used to unlock your notes. Because this happens on your device, the unencrypted data is never visible to the network or our servers.
What happens if I forget my password?
Because we don't store your password (or any "backdoor" keys), we cannot reset your password. If lost, your notes remain encrypted and mathematically impossible for anyone to recover. We strongly advise using a reliable password manager.
Can I sync across devices?
Yes. Your encrypted "blobs" are synced via our secure cloud. When you log in on a new device, your browser uses your password to locally decrypt the data. Sync is real-time and automatic.

Features & Data

How does Version History work?
VeroNote automatically saves a "snapshot" of your note whenever you make significant changes. Free users can restore versions from the last 7 days, while Pro users have access to an unlimited history.
How do I backup or export my notes?
Go to Settings > Data to export your entire library. Your data is exported as an encrypted JSON file, which you can keep as a personal backup or import back into VeroNote at any time.
Can I use VeroNote offline?
Yes. Once you have logged in, VeroNote caches your vault locally. You can continue to write and edit notes without an internet connection. Your changes will automatically sync to the cloud once you are back online.
