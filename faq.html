Commonly Asked Questions

Find quick answers about security, features, and your KryptNote account.

General

What is KryptNote?
KryptNote is a secure, end-to-end encrypted note-taking application. It is designed with a zero-knowledge architecture, meaning only you can read your notes, even we cannot access them.
Is KryptNote truly free to use?
Yes. Our Free plan allows you to create unlimited notes, access 7 days of version history, and use end-to-end encryption forever. The Pro plan unlocks unlimited version history and advanced AI features.
Do you have a mobile app?
KryptNote is primarily a web application optimized for mobile browsers. Dedicated mobile apps for iOS and Android are planned for future development and will be available to all users.

Account & Security

How is my data protected?
We use AES-GCM 256-bit end-to-end encryption. Your password is used to derive a Master Key, which then unlocks your Data Key. Your notes are encrypted locally in your browser *before* being sent to our servers. We never store your password or unencrypted notes.
What if I forget my password?
Due to the zero-knowledge nature of the service, we cannot recover your password. If you forget your password, your notes will be permanently inaccessible. We strongly recommend using a password manager.
Can I export my data?
Yes. You can export all your notes as a single,encrypted text file or as an JSON file from the settings menu. This ensures you always have a personal backup.

Usage & Features

How does the Version History work?
Version History automatically saves snapshots of your note when you navigate away from the editor or switch notes. The Free plan keeps versions for the last 7 days. The Pro plan keeps all versions indefinitely.
What are the "Advanced AI Features" in the Pro plan?
The Pro plan's AI features include tools like automatic summarization, grammar and spelling correction, and text expansion, all running on encrypted data where possible, ensuring privacy is maintained.
Can I use KryptNote offline?
When logged in, KryptNote uses local storage to cache your encrypted notes. This allows you to view and edit existing notes even without an internet connection. Changes are synced once you come back online.
