Frequently Asked Questions

Can I switch between plans anytime?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings. Changes take effect immediately.
Is my data secure with encryption?
Absolutely. We use industry-standard end-to-end encryption (AES-256) to ensure your notes remain private and secure. Even we cannot read them.
What happens if I cancel Pro?
Your account will revert to the Free plan. You will keep your existing notes, but you won't be able to create new custom categories or access file versions older than 7 days.
